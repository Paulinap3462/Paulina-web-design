There are two types of electric guitars with differences in both looks and functions.

  • The Stratocaster
      • Has a slim shape with a double cutaway body design
      Mostly used for music genre such as rock, metal, and even indie!
      Has six strings
      has three single coil pickups
      has a tremolo system
    The Telecaster
      Has a more square like shape
      Has a single cutaway body design
      Telecaster does not have a tremolo system, limiting the genre of music you can play
      Many indie artists enjoy using telecasters
      Has two coil pickups
      cable jack is located on the side
